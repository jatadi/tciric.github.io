Clarity

Winner of the Travelers 2025 Best Senior Project Award at Trinity College

Project Overview

Clarity emerged from a simple yet powerful idea: what if we could preserve someone's voice while breaking down language barriers? Working with a talented team, I developed this AI-powered platform that goes beyond traditional translation. By maintaining a person's voice identity across languages, Clarity creates a more human, more personal connection in critical communication scenarios.

The Problem We Solved

In healthcare settings, effective communication can be a matter of life and death. For patients wearing CPAP masks or those who don't speak the local language, expressing urgent needs becomes challenging. Clarity addresses this by providing instant, voice-preserved translation that maintains the personal connection between patient and provider.

Recognition & Impact

Our work on Clarity has been recognized for its innovative approach and potential impact in healthcare:

  • Travelers 2025 Best Senior Project Award at Trinity College
  • Featured project at Trinity College STEM Project Symposium
  • Recognized for potential impact in healthcare communication

Key Features

Core Functionality

  • Voice Identity Preservation - Maintain personal connection across languages
  • Real-Time Translation - Instant communication in 9 languages
  • Offline Capability - Works reliably in any setting
  • Privacy-Focused - All processing done on-device

Technical Achievement

  • Swift & iOS Development - Native performance
  • On-Device AI - Fast, private processing
  • Voice Synthesis - Natural-sounding output
  • Low Latency - Under 1s translation time

Development Journey

Leading the development of Clarity taught me invaluable lessons about creating technology that truly impacts people's lives. From early prototypes to our award-winning final implementation, each challenge we overcame brought us closer to our goal of making human connection transcend language barriers.
